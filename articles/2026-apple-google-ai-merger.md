# Why Apple Buying Google for AI Won’t Happen — and Doesn’t Need To

The idea is tempting: Apple buys Google, absorbs world-class AI research and infrastructure, and “wins” AI.

In reality, it’s a regulatory non-starter and a strategic mismatch. Even if it were possible, Apple’s strongest advantage isn’t ownership of another giant — it’s integration: devices, silicon, operating systems, and distribution.

The AI era will be won by execution and ecosystem control, not mega-mergers.
